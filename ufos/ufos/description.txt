Add a UFO that allows the player to have a fly house.

Depends:
 * default (mod of standard game)
 * intllib (optional: https://github.com/minetest-mods/intllib) ← Internationalization library for Minetest.
 * minertrade (optional: https://github.com/Lunovox/minertrade) ← Opens the carrier trunk of UFO that is interconnected with the Public ATM and the Personal Strongbox.
